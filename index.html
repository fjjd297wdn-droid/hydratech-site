Technology

HydraBox utilizes a compact thermoelectric system to condense water vapor from ambient air onto cooled surfaces. Our architecture focuses on high surface‑area contact, efficient heat exchange, and capillary‑assisted water collection, with an emphasis on portability and low maintenance.

  • Compact & Portable: Designed for homes, field teams, and remote communities.
  • Energy‑Aware: Optimized for battery and solar operation.
  • Modular: Scalable components for different climates and use‑cases.
  • Filtered Output: Integrated carbon filtration for clean, drinkable water.
At a Glance

Key elements of the system include multi‑surface condensation, precision thermal interfaces, and directional capillary flow to move water into a clean reservoir above the electronics compartment.

We are actively iterating on yields, materials, and control strategies to maximize water per watt.
