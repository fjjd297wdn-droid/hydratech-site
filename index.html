Turning Air Into Water — Anywhere on Earth.

Hydratech Industries is building next‑generation atmospheric water generation (AWG) systems to make clean water accessible, portable, and sustainable — starting with HydraBox, our compact field‑ready device delivering 2–5 L/day from ambient air using solar or AC power.

From day one, our mission has been to deliver life‑sustaining water to disaster zones, conflict regions, and remote communities — while advancing the frontier of atmospheric water tech.

Technology, engineered for the real world.

We combine thermoelectric cooling, optimized airflow, and intelligent power management to maximize liters per watt — without fragile, hard‑to‑service parts.

Energy‑Efficient AWG

Condensation stack tuned for high yield per watt with adaptive fan curves and heat‑exchange design.

Field‑Ready Durability

Rugged enclosure, replaceable filters, and simple serviceability for off‑grid and emergency deployments.

Flexible Power

Runs on portable solar + battery pack or standard AC, with smart power budgeting for day/night cycles.

Low‑Maintenance Design

Few moving parts, accessible consumables, and modular components reduce downtime and cost.

Flagship

HydraBox — compact water from air.

A portable, human‑carryable unit producing 2–5 liters/day in typical conditions. Designed for rapid deployment in disaster relief, military, and remote communities.
